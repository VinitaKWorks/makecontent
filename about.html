About Us

We love creating content, and we love supporting other content creators as well. Media production in America outside of Los Angeles and New York is tough. This is why we formed this organization dedicated to gathering all kinds of independent media production crews. We gather crew members for all kinds of media, from animations, to fictional films, to documentaries. Our goal is to help anyone pursue their media passions.

Our team also evaluates submitted forms regularly to make sure that the posters are trustworthy and that our platform users will be working with the right people. Please check our “Posts” tab to see who is currently calling for performers and creators. You may also fill out our form in our “Contact” tab to get started in content creation.

You may submit forms for hiring writers, videographers, editors, performers, or anyone else.
